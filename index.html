About Old Laurentian RFC

Old Laurentian RFC has been a cornerstone of local rugby for decades, fostering a tradition of excellence both on and off the field. Our club is built on the values of respect, integrity, passion, solidarity, and discipline - the core principles that guide everything we do.

From our humble beginnings to our current position as a respected member of the rugby community, we have always prioritized the development of players at every level. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or new to the sport, Old Laurentian RFC welcomes you to be part of our rugby family.

Our commitment extends beyond match day, with active community involvement, youth development programs, and a strong social calendar that brings together players, supporters, and families throughout the year.
